<i>Whispers of Stone and Sky - </i>Haiku Meditations on Indian Monuments – in Esperanto</b><p>Step into the quiet, sun-warmed courtyards of India’s ancient monuments through a sequence of lyrical haiku—each one written in Esperanto and inspired by a specific place. From the flickering lamps of Madurai temples to the shifting reflections of Udaipur’s lakes, this collection captures the poetic essence of history, memory, and place.</p>

<p>Crafted for readers at the A1–A2 level of Esperanto, these 17-syllable verses invite you to experience both language and landscape in a gentle, meditative way.</p>

<p>This volume continues the <i>mjolnir_poeto</i> project—an exploration of artificial intelligence, human creativity, and the power of constructed languages to unlock artistic potential for beginners and polyglots alike.</p>

<p>🕊️ For language learners.<br>
📜 For lovers of poetry.<br>
🕌 For those who see monuments as vessels of time.</p>
